Hi! I'm Sage.

A 14-year-old author, singer-songwriter, and full-time adventurer currently living on a sailboat exploring the Caribbean Sea. Originally from Southern California and Bend, Oregon.

For the past three years I've been exploring new places, writing fantasy novels, and making music along the way. When I'm not dreaming up magical worlds, you'll find me free diving, playing guitar, writing songs, or hanging out with my friends.
