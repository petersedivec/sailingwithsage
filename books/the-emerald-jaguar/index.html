Official Summary

Sage Sedino's world is about to change forever.

Eleven-year-old Sage and her siblings are suddenly swept away to a mysterious new galaxy — one brimming with magic, enchanted creatures, and hidden secrets. As they explore this strange realm, they discover they've inherited extraordinary powers of their own. But as their abilities grow stronger, Sage uncovers a chilling truth: a ruthless rebel group has unleashed a shapeshifting jaguar, wreaking havoc in the human world.

Sage, her siblings, and their cousins must harness their newfound powers to stop the shapeshifting jaguar and the dark forces controlling it — before it's too late. But as they dig deeper into the galaxy's mysteries, they uncover shocking truths about their family — some that will strengthen their resolve, and others that could tear their world apart.

Packed with adventure, unexpected twists, and heart-pounding action, this magical journey will leave you on the edge of your seat, eagerly awaiting what comes next.

Is this book right for your family?

It really depends on the family — but here's what to base your decision on. For reading level, it's probably 8 and up for the average child. Children ages 6 and up can enjoy the story too, though the book does include battle scenes, prisons, death, and attacks. There are no swear words, though "dumb" and "stupid" are used playfully in a few places. Make the call as a family — and if you have any questions, feel free to contact me.
