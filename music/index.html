My Story in Songs

I wrote my first song at eight years old. It wasn't very good — but it was mine. Music became my way of turning feelings I couldn't quite say out loud into something I could share.

I play acoustic guitar and piano, and I write about everything: friendships, the places I travel, moments on the boat, the people who shape me. Sometimes a song starts as a voice memo recorded on a ski slope. Sometimes it comes from a late-night Zoom call with my best friend Lyla.

All my songs are posted on Instagram at @songs.by.sage — I'd love for you to listen.
